Expected format: An Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file with:

  • Column A (or first column): Customer names
  • Remaining columns: Monthly revenue per customer
  • Row 1: Header row with dates (e.g., 1/31/2024, 2/28/2024, ...)
  • Rows 2+: One row per customer with their monthly revenue values
  • Use 0 or leave blank for months with no revenue

Revenue values should be monthly (MRR).
